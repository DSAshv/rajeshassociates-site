What We Do

Complete Legal Solutions For Property Matters

Specialised legal services designed around your real estate and property requirements

Legal Opinions & Title Investigation

Detailed title search and written legal opinions to support informed property decisions.

Title Clearance & Rectification

Identifying and resolving title defects to ensure your property is bank-ready and marketable.

Legal Documentation & Drafting

Drafting and vetting of sale deeds, gift deeds, partition deeds, and all related legal documents.

Property & Document Registration

End-to-end registration support for properties, marriages, firms, and partnership deeds.

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Strategic representation in civil and property disputes, injunctions, and court proceedings.

Online & Remote Consultation

RERA Complaints & Disputes

Expert handling of complaints and disputes before the Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA).

Police & Cyber Crime Complaints

Drafting and filing of police and cyber crime complaints related to property fraud.

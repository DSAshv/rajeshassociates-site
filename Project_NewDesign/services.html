Practice Areas

Our Complete Range of Legal Services

Comprehensive support across property, registration, and litigation matters.

Legal Opinions & Title Investigation

We thoroughly examine parent documents, encumbrance certificates, revenue records, and government approvals to deliver a clear written legal opinion on your proposed transaction.

Our title investigation covers complete ownership history, potential legal risks, and practical recommendations — empowering you to decide with confidence.

Title Clearance & Rectification

We identify defects or gaps in property titles and advise on corrective measures — including rectification deeds, release deeds, family settlements, and supplementary documents.

Our goal is to make the property fully marketable and acceptable to banks, buyers, and government authorities.

Legal Documentation & Deed Drafting

Expert drafting and vetting of sale deeds, settlement deeds, partition deeds, gift deeds, powers of attorney, and related legal documents.

Every document is crafted to accurately reflect your intentions and protect your long-term interests.

Property & Document Registration

Full support for property registration at the Sub-Registrar Office — from online token booking and document preparation to on-site assistance.

We also handle registration of marriages, firms, and partnership deeds.

Litigation & Property Disputes

Court representation in civil suits involving property disputes, injunctions, specific performance, partition matters, and other real estate litigation.

We analyse the facts, identify the most effective legal remedy, and guide you through every stage of the court process.

Property Verification & Field Inspection

On-ground verification of property location, boundaries, access roads, and cross-checks against government records and approved layouts.

This ensures that what you see on paper matches what actually exists on the ground.

Virtual Legal Consultation

Connect with our advocates via phone or video call. Share documents digitally for quick review.

Perfect for NRIs, outstation clients, and busy professionals who need prompt, clear legal guidance.

RERA Complaints & Regulatory Matters

We assist in drafting complaints, preparing responses, and filing documentation before the Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA).

We help both homebuyers and promoters understand their rights, obligations, and remedies under RERA.

Police Complaints & Cyber Crime Cases

Drafting and filing police complaints related to property fraud, cheating, and cyber offences linked to real estate transactions.

We advise you on the appropriate legal forum and the supporting documentation needed.

Who We Are

M/s.RAJESH ASSOCIATE & ADVOCATES is a Chennai-based law firm dedicated to real estate and property law. We help buyers, sellers, builders, investors, and families make well-informed property decisions.

From title verification and documentation to registration and litigation, we safeguard your rights at every stage. Our strength lies in combining deep legal knowledge with practical, on-the-ground experience in the Chennai property market.

What We Stand For

We believe legal services must be transparent, accessible, and solution-driven. Every opinion, notice, and document is prepared with care — ensuring you fully understand the legal position and any associated risks.

  • Honest and straightforward legal opinions
  • Meticulous document verification before every transaction
  • Client-first advice tailored to your goals
  • Timely updates with clear, jargon-free communication

Our Working Process

Whether you're buying your first home, settling ancestral property matters, or resolving a dispute, we follow a clear and structured process:

  • Understanding your requirements and property background
  • Collecting and reviewing all available documents
  • Conducting thorough searches and verifications
  • Providing a written legal opinion or action plan
  • Supporting you end-to-end through registration or litigation

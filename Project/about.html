More About Rajesh Associates & Advocates

Dedicated property law specialists offering clear, practical and reliable legal guidance.

Who We Are

M/s. Rajesh Associates & Advocates is a Chennai-based law firm focused on real estate and property matters. We assist buyers, sellers, builders, investors and families in making confident property decisions.

From title verification and documentation to registration and litigation, we work to protect your rights at every stage. Our approach combines deep legal knowledge with practical, on-ground experience in the Chennai property market.

Our Values

We believe that legal services should be transparent, accessible and solution-oriented. Every opinion, notice and document is prepared with care so that you clearly understand the legal position and possible risks.

  • Honest, straightforward legal opinions
  • Thorough verification of documents before any transaction
  • Client-focused advice based on your goals
  • Timely updates and clear communication

How We Work With You

Whether you are purchasing your first home, regularising ancestral property, or resolving a dispute, we follow a structured process:

  • Understanding your requirement and property background
  • Collecting and reviewing all available documents
  • Conducting necessary searches and verifications
  • Sharing a written opinion or action plan
  • Supporting you through registration or litigation as required
